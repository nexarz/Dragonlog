About

Mount your phone in the boat with a clear view of the sky for best GPS. Strokes are detected from phone motion — adjust sensitivity if readings seem off. All data is stored locally on your device — nothing is uploaded.

CALIBRATE DPS

Paddle a known distance to learn this profile's distance per stroke. The more accurate the distance, the better the estimate.
